How to play
  1. One person creates a lobby and shares the code or link with friends.
  2. The host picks the roles in play and how long Night and Day last, then starts the hunt.
  3. Each Night, the Werewolves secretly choose someone to eliminate, while other special roles quietly use their own powers.
  4. Each Day, everyone discusses and votes to eliminate a suspect. If there is a tie, a revote happens between only the tied players.
  5. When you die you become a spectator — you can see everything but cannot act or speak to living players.
  6. When eliminated, you choose whether to reveal your role or keep it hidden.
  7. Tap your role badge any time to see your role again. Use 📜 for the event log and 👥 for the full roster.
