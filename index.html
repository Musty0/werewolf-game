How to play
  1. One person creates a lobby and shares the code or link with friends.
  2. The host picks the roles in play and how long Night and Day last, then starts the hunt.
  3. Each Night, the Mafia secretly choose someone to eliminate, while any other special roles in play quietly use their own powers.
  4. Each Day, everyone discusses out loud and votes to eliminate a suspect.
  5. Town wins by voting out every Mafia member, and Mafia wins once they equal the town in number. Some roles win alone in other ways — check 🐺 Roles for details.
  6. Tap your role badge any time to see your role again. Use 📜 for the town crier's log and 👥 for the full roster.
